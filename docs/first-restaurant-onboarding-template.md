## Staff Quick Start

Cashier:
1. Open staff login.
2. Select Cashier.
3. Enter restaurant username and PIN.
4. Collect payment.
5. Mark order paid.

Kitchen:
1. Open staff login.
2. Select Kitchen.
3. Enter restaurant username and PIN.
4. Work oldest paid order first.
5. Move order through Preparing, Almost Ready, Ready, Completed.

Admin:
1. Open staff login.
2. Select Admin.
3. Enter restaurant username and PIN.
4. Add/edit menu items.
5. Mark sold-out items quickly during rush.
6. Open or close ordering when needed.
