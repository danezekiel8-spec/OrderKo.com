# Staff Pilot SOP

## Cashier

1. Open `/staff/login`.
2. Select Cashier.
3. Enter the restaurant username and cashier PIN.
4. Search or scan the customer's visible order number.
5. Confirm the amount with the customer.
6. Take payment at the counter.
7. Tap Mark Paid.

Unpaid orders do not appear in the kitchen queue. Cancel only when the customer confirms they no longer want the order.

## Kitchen

1. Open `/staff/login`.
2. Select Kitchen.
3. Enter the restaurant username and kitchen PIN.
4. Work from the oldest paid order first.
5. Tap Preparing when work starts.
6. Tap Almost Ready when the order is nearly complete.
7. Tap Ready when the customer can collect it.
8. Tap Completed after pickup.

## Admin

Use `/admin` to add or edit menu items, mark sold out items, update restaurant details, and pause ordering by closing the restaurant.

## If Wi-Fi Drops

1. Keep visible active orders on screen.
2. Switch to paper ordering at the counter.
3. Do not refresh staff tablets unless needed.
4. When internet returns, check `/staff/cashier` and `/staff/kitchen`.
5. Reconcile any paper orders before resuming QR ordering.

## If An Order Looks Missing

1. Search by order number in cashier.
2. Ask whether the customer saw a confirmation number.
3. If no confirmation number appeared, use paper order fallback.
4. If payment was already taken, manually prepare and reconcile after rush hour.
